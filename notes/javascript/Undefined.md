It is the most common standard that JS employs to represent the absence of a [[Value]].

It is the [[Return value]] of `console.log`:
```
> console.log("Hello, World!")
Hello, World!
= undefined
```
Also arises when declaring [[Variable]]s without a [[Value]], or from [[Function call]]s that don't have a `return` [[Statement]] in their [[Function definition]].
