Here's a simplified list of precedence for the most common [[Logical operator]]s and [[Comparison]] [[Expression]]s (from high to low):
- `<=`, `<`, `>`, `>=` - Comparison
- `===`, `!==`, `==`, `!=` - Equality
- `&&` - Logical AND
- `||` - Logical OR

In practice, use **parenthesis** if there's any doubts whatsoever about the precedence (parenthesis **override** precedence).
