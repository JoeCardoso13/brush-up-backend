The primitive data [[Type]]s are:
* [[String]]
* [[Number]]
* [[Undefined]]
* [[Null]]
* [[Boolean]]
([[ES6]] introduces Symbol and BigInt)

[[Primitive]] [[Value]]s are the building blocks of JavaScript. They are indivisible and **immutable**, sometimes referred to as *atomic*.
