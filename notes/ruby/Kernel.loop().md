The primordial example of [[Iteration]] [[Method]]. It takes a [[Block]] and repeatedly executes it, until if finds a [[break Statement]], a `return` keyword, or other statement that exits the loop. Its [[Return value]] will then be determined by the [[Expression]] used to exit the loop.
