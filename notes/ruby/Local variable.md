The most common of the [[Types of variables]]. Declared without special character nor capitalizing:
```ruby
var = 'I must be passed around to cross scope boundaries.'
```
They will obey all [[Variable Scope]] rules.

The [[Kernel.local_variables]] [[Method]] can be used to find all local variables available within the current [[Variable Scope]].
