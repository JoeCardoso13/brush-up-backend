Their [[Return value]] is **always** a [[Boolean]].

Used in control flow, with [[Control Expression]]s, based on the [[Return value]] of comparisons. On a high-level, it is when operators like `>`, `>=`,  `==`, etc are associated with [[if-else Expression]]s type. On a lower level these operators are, in fact, built-in [[Method]]s. Their precise definition depends upon the class of the calling [[Object]].

They can be concatenated in [[Logical expression]]s.
