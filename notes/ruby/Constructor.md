Constructors are special **procedures** in [[OOP]] that are run every time an [[Object]] [[Instantiation]] happens.

In Ruby, the [[Initialize method]] is the constructor. Calling the [[New method]] from a [[Class]] triggers the [[Method call]] of the [[Initialize method]] of that [[Class]].
