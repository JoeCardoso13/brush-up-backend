The following [[Method]]s are called by an [[Object]] that is **not**  a [[Collection]] - defined by **not** including the [[Enumerable module]] - and returns an [[Array]], the quintessential [[Collection]] class.

* [[String.split]]
* [[String.chars]]
* [[String.codepoints]]
* [[String.partition]]
* [[Integer.digits]]
