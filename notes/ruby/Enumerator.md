The enumerator [[Class]] works along with the [[Enumerable module]] to provide for [[Iteration]]s in Ruby.

Most [[Method]]s have two forms: a [[Block]] form where the contents are evaluated for each item in the enumeration, and a non-[[Block]] form which returns a new [`Enumerator`](https://docs.ruby-lang.org/en/3.2/Enumerator.html) wrapping the iteration.
