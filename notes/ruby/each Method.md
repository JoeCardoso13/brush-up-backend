It's the simplest of the [[Collection]] [[Method]]s, it runs the code in the [[Block]] for each element of the [[Collection]] and its [[Return value]] is simply the [[Collection]] itself (**not** a copy). It is preferred over the [[for Loop]]. 

It forms the basis of a number of more specialized examples of [[Collection]] [[Method]]s and also some [[Control Expression]]s that can perform [[Iteration]]s, such as [[for Loop]], [[while Loop]] and [[until Loop]]. These are **all** implemented on top of the [[each Method]].
