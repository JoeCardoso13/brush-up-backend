It is the [[Class method]] through which [[Class]]es make [[Instantiation]] of [[Object]]s. 

When a [[Class]] calls the *new method*, it triggers its [[Initialize method]], i.e. runs its [[Method definition]]. Hence the new [[Method call]] must have the correct number of arguments as specified in the [[Initialize method]] definition.
