### `/concatenated pattern/`

Is the **default** structure of [[Regex]]. Each character from the [[String]] is treated as a concatenated **pattern**. The regex `/cat/`, for instance, consists of the concatenation of the `c`, `a`, and `t` **patterns**, and matches any [[String]] that contains a `c` followed by an `a` followed by a `t`.
