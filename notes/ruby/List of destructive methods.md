Here is a list of some destructive [[Method]]s to be aware of:
1. [[Array.shift]]
2. [[Array.push]] or [[Array.shovel]]
3. [[Array.pop]]
4. [[Array.unshift]]
5. [[Array.concat]]
6. [[Array.flatten!]]
7. [[Collection setters]]
8. [[String.concat]] or [[String.shovel]]
9. [[String.slice!]]
10. [[String.insert]]
11. [[String substitution method]] ending in '!'
