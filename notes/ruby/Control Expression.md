* Will *always* have **a** [[Return value]] corresponding to the last [[Expression]] executed.
* They **do not** create new [[Variable Scope]] (**not** to be confused with [[Layered Scope]] from [[Block]] execution or [[Self-contained Scope]] from [[Method invocation]]).

Ruby's control expressions are (6-9 perform **iterations**):
1. [[if-else Expression]]
2. [[Ternary if]]
3. [[unless Expression]]
4. [[Modifier if and unless]]
5. [[case Expression]]
6. [[while Loop]]
7. [[until Loop]]
8. [[for Loop]]
9. [[Modifier while and until]]
10. [[break Statement]]
11. [[next Statement]]
